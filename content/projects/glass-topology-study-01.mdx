# Glass Topology Study 01

![Glass Topology Render](/images/glass-topology-01.jpg)

## Render Parameters

- **Refractive Index**: 1.5 (glass)
- **Dispersion**: Enabled (Abbe number: 55)
- **Samples**: 2048
- **Resolution**: 3840×2160

## Process Notes

This study explores how light interacts with complex topological surfaces when given glass-like properties. The challenge was balancing visual interest with the quiet, contemplative aesthetic I'm after.

### Technical Details

- Renderer: Cycles (Blender)
- Surface: Gyroid minimal surface with thickness variation
- Lighting: HDRI + subtle rim light
- Post: Minimal color grading, slight bloom

The dispersion creates subtle rainbow artifacts at the edges—just enough to catch the eye without being gaudy.
